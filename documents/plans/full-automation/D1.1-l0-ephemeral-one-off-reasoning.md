## Reader narrative
<!-- prose-source: agent plane-d 2026-06-28 -->

L0 is the bottom rung of the promotion ladder: reasoning that lives only in the current agent turn—useful once, not yet captured as a reusable artifact. Every pursuit session starts here for novel problems; the platform queue exists so repeated L0 patterns can graduate upward instead of being re-derived every sprint.

L0 work is legitimate product progress but a liability for scale. When the same manual sequence, explanation, or routing choice appears twice, enqueue a promotion item with target_level above L0. ## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. On each pursuit wake for novel work, the conductor and workers operate at L0—ephemeral reasoning confined to the current turn until the outcome is captured as a reusable artifact on the promotion ladder.
2. L0 progress is valid product delivery but creates scale risk; when the same routing choice, manual sequence, or explanation repeats across turns, pursuit enqueues a `state.platform.promotion_queue` item with `target_level` above L0 per [D2.1](D2.1-index.md) triggers.
3. The autopilot scheduler interleaves platform turns with product work so repeated L0 patterns graduate into playbooks, scripts, or catalog entries instead of being re-derived every sprint.
4. The conductor dual-writes enqueue events and current ladder level to state.json and journal so operators can audit what remains ephemeral versus promoted.
5. If a pattern recurs twice without promotion enqueue, or L0 reasoning contradicts an existing playbook without reconcile-stale, pursuit stops at H2 until the platform backlog is corrected.
