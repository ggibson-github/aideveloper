# A6.3: operator observe without unblocking loop

**Parent:** [A6-index](A6-index.md) · **Branch A** · **Vision §3** · **Release:** v2.23

## Reader narrative
<!-- prose-source: agent plane-a 2026-06-28 -->

Operators may inspect dashboards, journals, evidence logs, and queue depth while pursuit remains blocked without that inspection counting as “unblocking.” Read-only observation must not clear H2, advance `next_action`, or waive gates.

This separation prevents accidental resume when an operator opens STATUS to diagnose a failure. Explicit unblock actions—supplying credentials, answering a blocking question, approving H3—are distinct events recorded in journal Q&A. Autonomous loops remain paused until preflight passes on the actual fix.

## Purpose

## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. Operators may read [A6.1](A6.1-notify-dashboard-status-webhook-phase-complete.md) dashboard, `STATUS.md`, journal/progress.md, evidence logs, and platform queue depth while pursuit remains BLOCKED—read-only inspection never clears H2, advances `next_action`, or waives `gates_pending`.
2. [A5.1](A5.1-continue-resume-pursuit-if-not-blocked.md) and [A5.2](A5.2-continue-not-approval-self-gate-h1-h3-only.md) treat observation separately from resume: opening STATUS to diagnose a failure does not count as Continue or implicit gate approval.
3. Explicit unblock actions—supplying credentials, answering a `blocking_questions` entry, recording H3 approval or rejection—are distinct journal Resolved Q&A events that preflight must see before [A2.2](A2.2-if-ready-execute-one-pipeline-step.md) may run again.
4. Under [A3.2](A3.2-goal-autopilot-until-goal-verify-or-hard-block.md) and [A3.4](A3.4-sdk-daemon-run-local-pipeline-24-7.md) unattended modes, the loop stays paused at [A4.1](A4.1-stop-human-h1-h2-h3.md) H2 until preflight passes on the actual fix, not when the operator merely acknowledged a digest per [A6.2](A6.2-notify-digest-on-h2-blocker-not-every-step.md).
5. If an operator action is ambiguous (e.g. a comment without answering the blocker), pursuit dual-writes H2 with the unresolved question—observation alone never transitions `goal.state` from blocked to pursuing.
