## Reader narrative
<!-- prose-source: agent plane-b 2026-06-28 -->

The conductor is genius-tier and thin: merge worker summaries, run S0 preflight, choose next_action, spawn workers, and route platform queue drains—it does not implement large task cards inline when `spawn_workers` is true.

## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. The genius-tier conductor merges worker summaries each turn, reads route-tier output, and chooses `next_action` without implementing large task cards inline when `spawn_workers` is true.
2. On the interleaved schedule from Plane D, the conductor may peek or drain one platform promotion queue item per K product steps via [B4.2](B4.2-platform-promotion-queue-peek-drain.md) while product pursuit stays on the main stack.
3. Only the conductor dual-writes `journal/progress.md` and `journal/state.json` after workers return; workers never clear gates, approve design, or advance `next_action` on their own.
4. The conductor spawns economy workers with bounded `allowed_reads` from task cards or Librarian output and keeps platform workers confined to queue item ids without mutating consumer goal scope.
5. If merge would skip S0 preflight, corrupt dual-write, or drain platform work that blocks the active product goal, pursuit stops at H2 until routing and queue policy reconcile.
