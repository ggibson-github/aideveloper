## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. After substantial implement diffs or when pack policy flags risk, the conductor spawns readonly reviewer workers—Bugbot for branch changes, security-review for touch paths and dependencies.
2. Reviewer findings return as bounded summaries; reviewers do not dual-write pursuit state and cannot clear gates or advance `next_action` on their own.
3. Review complements [B2.4](B2.4-verifier-tool-operator-evidence.md) evidence: verify-router proves commands ran; reviewers detect mistake classes from Plane G before push.
4. In strict mode the conductor blocks git-workflow until reviewer pass; in default self-gate mode findings may be recorded as waivable with expiry when automatable checks pass.
5. Critical or unresolved reviewer findings route to H2 or [B1.5](B1.5-s4-governance-escalation-h2-packaging.md) rather than merging to main or requesting H3 sign-off.
