## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. On scheduled platform turns per [SEC-13](SEC-13-pursuit-flow.md) interleaving, the conductor runs S0 scripts first—queue peek, promotion apply, staleness reconcile, manifest validation—before any economy worker interprets platform debt.
2. Economy platform workers from `model-policy.json` execute [B2.6](B2.6-platform-worker-promotion-queue-parallel-slot.md) promotion items under bounded contracts; clerical queue mutations stay on S0 or the conductor per [B1.1](B1.1-s0-deterministic-mandatory-first.md).
3. Platform workers use the same evidence discipline as product workers—[B2.4](B2.4-verifier-tool-operator-evidence.md) verify where required—but write outcomes to platform queue state and pack fragments, not consumer feature branches.
4. Product `next_action` remains primary; platform turns dequeue only when [D3.1](D3.1-1-platform-turn-per-k-product-turns.md) schedule and [B4.2](B4.2-platform-promotion-queue-peek-drain.md) peek/drain policy allow, so self-improvement does not starve the active goal.
5. If S0 platform scripts fail or a worker touches consumer goal scope, the conductor halts the platform turn and routes to H2—never marking promotion items done without verification evidence.
