## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cli-composer-2.5 2026-06-28 -->

1. Before spawning workers, the conductor runs Librarian (readonly) and receives at most five `allowed_reads` paths derived from `state.json`, the active task card, and scoped playbooks per [F6.2](F6.2-role-allowed-reads-scoped-playbooks-lane-tasks.md).
2. Spawn contracts pass `allowed_reads` unchanged to economy workers; workers must not open paths outside the list except through declared tool output captured as evidence.
3. When context is insufficient, compose-first routing ([E2.1](E2.1-compose-resolve-capability-needed.md)–[E2.4](E2.4-compose-plan-task-card-components.md)) ranks catalog components and playbooks instead of expanding the read set beyond the cap.
4. The genius-tier conductor may read INDEX files and task cards beyond worker bounds but still prefers bounded briefing over loading entire design trees during orchestration turns.
5. Scope-bleed attempts—workers reading forbidden paths or conductors omitting `allowed_reads` from spawn contracts—fail closed as [G5.2](G5.2-mistake-scope-creep-allowed-reads-task-card.md) violations and stop at H2 until spawn contracts are corrected and dual-written.
