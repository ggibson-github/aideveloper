## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. At H1 program instantiation or pack selection, the conductor writes pack_id, active_role, template-pack version, and workstream lease refs into state.json company block—mirroring the journal Company section on dual-write.
2. When [B5.1](B5.1-active-role-from-template-pack.md) signals a role switch mid-goal, the conductor records the decision in journal before updating active_role; workers spawned thereafter inherit permissions from [B5.2](B5.2-role-to-pipeline-id-skills-tool-permissions.md) pack schema.
3. Program-mode pursuits load integration manifest and artifact graph pointers from the company block so [C4](C4-index.md) orchestration reads consistent cross-stream contracts.
4. pack_id null preserves default repo harness pipelines; non-null pack_id overrides pipeline_id and verify suites per [F1](F1-index.md) without mixing pack fragments.
5. If company block drifts from journal Company on dual-write, or manifest refs point to stale nodes, preflight halts at H2 until conductor reconcile restores consistency.
