2. 4. Mature patterns promoted from product pursuit must land as pack fragments via Plane D export ([D4.6](D4.6-platform-work-pack-fragment-export.md)); consumer-only role or pipeline edits cannot satisfy maturity claims or goal_verify suites tied to the pack ceiling.
5. If S0 or validate-workflow detects pack-defined authority living outside `template-packs/`, pursuit stops at H2 with the offending path recorded in journal and state.json until operators restore ceiling compliance or approve a pack update at H1.
