## Reader narrative
<!-- prose-source: agent plane-f 2026-06-28 -->

Company autopilot rotates `state.company.active_role` based on ready work in lane queues—not fixed round-robin. When a role lane has unblocked tasks and leases available, conductor switches active_role, updates spawn contracts, and schedules pursuit for that persona ([A3.3](A3.3-company-autopilot-multi-goal-role-workstreams.md)).

Rotation must release held lane leases before role switch (complete-work-order). Role switch updates tool permissions ([F6.3](F6.3-role-tool-permissions-mcp-cli-allowlist.md)) and allowed_reads ([F6.2](F6.2-role-allowed-reads-scoped-playbooks-lane-tasks.md)) without merging permissions across roles. Conductor identity persists across switches ([F6.1](F6.1-role-mapping-conductor-context-switch-active-role.md)).

## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cli-composer-2.5 2026-06-28 -->

1. When company mode instantiates the data-platform template pack, [B5.1](B5.1-active-role-from-template-pack.md) sets `active_role` to analyst, data engineer, DBA, SRE, or governance reviewer and binds each to pipeline stages defined in [F4.2](F4.2-data-platform-pipelines-ingest-model-deploy-monitor.md).
2. Schema approval and regulated governance steps route to the governance reviewer role under pack `strict_hitl` policy—surfacing H2 blockers that [A6.2](A6.2-notify-digest-on-h2-blocker-not-every-step.md) digests without stalling unrelated lanes.
3. Production deploy and infrastructure commands execute only under the SRE `active_role` using tool permissions from [F6.3](F6.3-role-tool-permissions-mcp-cli-allowlist.md); other roles fail closed if deploy verbs appear in their spawn contract.
4. The genius-tier conductor rotates `active_role` across parallel workstreams per [F2.3](F2.3-company-active-role-rotates-ready-work.md) and [B5.4](B5.4-conductor-stays-workers-swap-role-context.md), injecting role-scoped `allowed_reads` from [F6.2](F6.2-role-allowed-reads-scoped-playbooks-lane-tasks.md) into each worker spawn.
5. If `active_role` drifts from pack YAML bindings or a role lacks pipeline permissions for the current `next_action`, `validate-workflow.py` stops pursuit at H2 until the pack fragment reconciles.
