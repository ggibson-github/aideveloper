## Reader narrative

## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. When journal-keeper records an H2 blocker or requests H3 sign-off, the runtime may emit an optional webhook or email per [A6.2](A6.2-notify-digest-on-h2-blocker-not-every-step.md)—routine pursuit steps and phase completions do not trigger outbound notify.
2. Notify payloads carry goal_id, structured blocker reason, and suggested operator action so recipients can act without opening the full journal.
3. Delivery runs outside the critical pursuit path; webhook or SMTP failure logs locally and pursuit continues without waiting for operator acknowledgment.
4. Strict environments may disable outbound notify in state or pack config; operators still observe status via [I5.1](I5.1-runtime-notify-status-dashboard-generation.md) dashboard regeneration.
5. If an alert was missed, the journal Blockers section and H3 pending state remain authoritative—notify is observability only and never substitutes for [A6.3](A6.3-operator-observe-without-unblocking-loop.md) clearance to resume autopilot.
