The story at a glance

Autonomy with guardrails — not autonomy without proof.

Operators set intent once (H1). The system pursues through product and platform turns, routing work across S0–S4 capability tiers, composing from catalog before inventing, and stopping only for blockers (H2) or final sign-off (H3). Verification runs every implement batch; evidence is mandatory before pursuit continues.
