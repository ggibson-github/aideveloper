Duplicate tooling is inventing a second script, skill, or queue handler when catalog or platform queue already provides one—often after skipped compose-first (B4.3). Controls: divergence logs (B4.4), platform promotion queue, and conformance checks for duplicate command shapes.

New tooling requires catalog entry or promotion item, not ad hoc scripts/ forks. Duplicate verify or routing scripts confuse route-tier and validate-workflow expectations.
