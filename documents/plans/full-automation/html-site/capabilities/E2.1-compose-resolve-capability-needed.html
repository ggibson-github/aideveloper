E2.1 · Knowledge & composition plane

compose, resolve, capability, and needed

compose resolve capability needed

Before any S1+ work, compose must resolve which capability the turn needs: routing, implement, verify, design artifact, platform promotion. Resolution names the capability id and target catalog slice—not a vague "figure it out" prompt.

Librarian and conductor share this step; output drives E2.2 query and E2.3 ranking. Unresolved capability blocks spawn until catalog search completes. This implements mandatory compose-before-invent from B4.3 at the Plane E protocol layer.

flowchart TD

How it works

  1. Step 1After implement diffs land, S0 touch-path detection compares task card declared files and diff stats against pack-flagged risk surfaces—auth, crypto, credentials paths, and dependency manifests—not conductor memory.
  2. Step 2When paths match risk triggers., the conductor spawns a readonly security-review subagent; findings merge through the conductor, not direct journal writes.
  3. Step 3Strict packs may require security-review pass before git-workflow advances; default self-gate records waivable findings with expiry when automated checks pass.
  4. Step 4Blocking security findings hold pursuit at H2 until remediated, waived with evidence, or escalated per pack policy.
  5. Step 5If security-review is skipped while touch paths matched, preflight fails closed rather than pushing unreviewed risk surfaces.
