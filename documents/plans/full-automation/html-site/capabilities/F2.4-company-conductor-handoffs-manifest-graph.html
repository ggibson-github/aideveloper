F2.4 · Organization plane

company, conductor, handoffs, manifest, and graph

company conductor handoffs manifest graph

The conductor orchestrates cross-role handoffs using instantiated manifest.md plus artifact-graph.json—checking prerequisite nodes, updating handoff manifest sections, and spawning the next role lane when contracts satisfy. This implements pack integration contracts (F1.4) at runtime.

Handoffs are conductor-owned S3 merges when ambiguity exists; mechanical prerequisite checks are S0 via reconcile-artifact-graph. Failed handoff surfaces structured H2 with missing artifact id and suggested owner role. Pair with B5.3 and B5.4 for worker vs parent separation.

How it works

  1. Step 1At H1 or company-goal instantiation, F2.1 loads company.yaml and roles/*.yaml from the active template-pack, dual-writing company.pack_id, company.active_role, and program workstreams into state.json without forking a separate harness per employee.
  2. Step 2The conductor selects active_role from the pack. so each department turn runs under the correct org-chart context.
  3. Step 3When parallel lanes are ready, F2.2 spawns workstreams with lane leases while F2.3 rotates active_role across departments that have ready queue items—coordinating cross-team handoffs through the integration manifest, not ad-hoc journal edits.
  4. Step 4Under A3.3 company autopilot, pursuit continues across role and workstream switches until each nested goal reaches goal_verify or a hard block fires; roll-up status stays on parent goals in state.json.
  5. Step 5If pack instantiation fails, role bindings conflict, or lane leases collide, pursuit stops with a structured H2 blocker rather than silently running product work under the wrong role context.

Edge cases

  • Operator closes laptop mid-loop — state.json must resume from last good dual-write.
  • Concurrent manual edit to queue JSON — conductor reloads queue each wake; last writer wins with journal note.
  • Pack role handoff while lane lease held — complete-work-order releases lease before role switch.
  • Edge case F2.4 variant 4: verify state dual-write before continuing pursuit.
