worker and runs

worker runs

worker-runs.jsonl append-only audit records each subagent spawn: role, model_tier, allowed_reads, task id, timestamps, and outcome summary. The conductor logs spawns; workers do not self-log journal/state changes.

Audit trail supports cost review, debugging scope bleed, and J4 governance queries. Parallel program lanes produce interleaved lines—correlate by goal id and lane lease (C4.1). Retention policy belongs in operator pack; default keeps full session history locally.

How it works

  1. Step 1After H1 approves the plan, the conductor sets autopilot.active and loops pursuit without a per-session step ceiling.
  2. Step 2Each iteration runs S0 preflight then exactly one pipeline phase per A2.2.
  3. Step 3When scope is complete, pursuit triggers goal_verify per A2.4 instead of another implement task.
  4. Step 4The loop stops on goal_verify pass, a hard block from A4, or budget exhaustion from A1.4.
  5. Step 5On corrupt state or failed validate-workflow, pursuit halts at H2 rather than continuing with bad state.
