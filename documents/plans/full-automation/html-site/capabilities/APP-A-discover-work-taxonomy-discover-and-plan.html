APP-A-discover · Front matter & cross-cutting architecture

Work taxonomy discover and plan

discover work taxonomy discover and plan

Discover and plan work covers requirements elicitation (with H1 assist), research, architecture tradeoffs, and milestone planning. In a template-pack, each leaf maps to pipeline phases, verification commands, and playbooks—not informal chat.

Pack authors use this taxonomy slice to ensure planning roles produce approved artifacts the pursuit loop can consume without re-asking baseline questions.

flowchart TD
  trigger[Trigger condition] --> pre[Preconditions S0]
  pre --> exec[Execute step logic]
  exec --> verify[Verify output]
  verify -->|pass| done[Mark queue item done]
  verify -->|fail| h2[H2 blocker]

What this defines

  1. Step 1When next_action targets discover or plan phases (run spec-parser, program-scoper, requirements research), the conductor invokes the matching S2 skill with librarian-bounded allowed_reads. instead of ad hoc repo exploration.
  2. Step 2Elicitation and research turns produce machine-checkable artifacts—parsed requirements, open-question lists, tradeoff notes, and milestone sketches—that dual-write to journal and state.json for H1 review before design or implement phases start.
  3. Step 3At H1 the operator approves or amends the plan; journal-keeper records resolved Q&A, clears blocking questions, and sets next_action so A5.3 can enter goal autopilot without re-asking baseline discovery.
  4. Step 4Template-pack authors map this taxonomy slice to pipeline phases, verification commands, and playbooks in pack YAML so company autopilot spawns discover roles with the correct skills and catalog paths.
  5. Step 5If discovery ends with prose-only chat, unresolved blocking questions, or no approved plan artifacts, pursuit fails closed at H2—never advance to HLD, DD, or implement until machine-checkable outputs exist.
