platform turns economy plus S0 scripts

Platform turns combine economy workers with S0 scripts: queue peek, promotion apply, staleness reconcile, and manifest validation run deterministically before any model interprets platform debt.

Product turns remain primary; platform turns interleave on schedule (SEC-13 pursuit flow). Platform workers use the same evidence discipline as product workers but write to platform queue state and pack fragments, not consumer feature branches.

How it works

  1. Step 1On scheduled platform turns. interleaving, the conductor runs S0 scripts first—queue peek, promotion apply, staleness reconcile, manifest validation—before any economy worker interprets platform debt.
  2. Step 2Economy platform workers from model-policy.json execute B2.6 promotion items under bounded contracts; clerical queue mutations stay on S0 or the conductor.
  3. Step 3Platform workers use the same evidence discipline as product workers—B2.4 verify where required—but write outcomes to platform queue state and pack fragments, not consumer feature branches.
  4. Step 4Product next_action remains primary; platform turns dequeue only when D3.1 schedule and B4.2 peek/drain policy allow, so self-improvement does not starve the active goal.
  5. Step 5If S0 platform scripts fail or a worker touches consumer goal scope, the conductor halts the platform turn and routes to H2—never marking promotion items done without verification evidence.
