PLATFORM promotion queue peek drain

PLATFORM promotion_queue peek drain

Platform promotion queue peek/drain runs on a K-step cadence during pursuit: inspect head items, drain ready promotions with S0 scripts, defer blocked items with journal notes. Peek is cheap; drain commits artifacts and updates maturity.

By default the current project step and one self-improvement step from the queue run in turn—delivery does not pause entirely while the system works on itself, unless policy explicitly prioritizes clearing the improvement backlog first. Failed drain surfaces H2 when external repo access or pack authority is missing.

How it works

  1. Step 1During pursuit, the conductor peeks the Plane D promotion queue on a K-step cadence—inspect head items cheaply, defer blocked items with journal notes—before committing to drain.
  2. Step 2Drain runs S0 promotion apply scripts on ready items, commits artifacts, updates maturity ladders, and dual-writes platform queue state; peek alone never mutates the queue.
  3. Step 3By default one product step and one self-improvement step alternate so delivery continues while the system works on itself unless policy prioritizes clearing the improvement backlog first.
  4. Step 4B2.6 platform workers execute drained items under bounded contracts; failed drain surfaces H2 when external repo access or pack authority is missing.
  5. Step 5If drain marks promotion complete without verification evidence or mutates consumer next_action, preflight fails closed and the queue head is rolled back with an integrity note.
