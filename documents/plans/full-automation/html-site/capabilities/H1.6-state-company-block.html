H1.6 · Persistence & state plane

state, company, and block

state company block

The company block binds pack_id, active_role, template-pack version, and multi-goal workstream leases for company autopilot. Role switches mid-goal require journal-recorded conductor decision (B5.1); workers inherit role permissions from pack schema (B5.2).

Integration manifest and artifact graph refs live here for program mode (C4). pack_id null means default repo harness; instantiated packs override pipeline_id and verify suites per F1. Company block must stay consistent with journal Company section on dual-write.

How it works

  1. Step 1At H1 program instantiation or pack selection, the conductor writes pack_id, active_role, template-pack version, and workstream lease refs into state.json company block—mirroring the journal Company section on dual-write.
  2. Step 2When B5.1 signals a role switch mid-goal, the conductor records the decision in journal before updating active_role; workers spawned thereafter inherit permissions from B5.2 pack schema.
  3. Step 3Program-mode pursuits load integration manifest and artifact graph pointers from the company block so C4 orchestration reads consistent cross-stream contracts.
  4. Step 4pack_id null preserves default repo harness pipelines; non-null pack_id overrides pipeline_id and verify suites. without mixing pack fragments.
  5. Step 5If company block drifts from journal Company on dual-write, or manifest refs point to stale nodes, preflight halts at H2 until conductor reconcile restores consistency.
