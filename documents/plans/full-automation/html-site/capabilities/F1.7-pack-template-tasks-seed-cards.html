F1.7 · Organization plane

pack, template, tasks, seed, and cards

pack template tasks seed cards

template tasks/ seeds task cards per role—pre-written Components, Test commands, evidence types, and promotion_note stubs so instantiation does not start from empty TODOS. program-scoper or task-breakdown copies seeds into docs/features/ or program lanes with pack_id provenance.

Seed cards bind pack verify suites (F1.8) and role evidence rules (F6.4) at first implement turn. Seeds are templates, not completed work—evidence gates still apply. Platform promotion (D6.3) requires task cards reference promoted artifacts after they have been promoted from the improvement queue into lasting catalog entries.

How it works

  1. Step 1During pursuit, state.pursuit records mode (continue, goal_autopilot, company_autopilot), steps_total, capability_class, last_verify, evidence_files, and gates_pending—the live control surface S0 preflight reads before each turn.
  2. Step 2Autopilot modes increment steps_total each iteration until check-pipeline-blocked, budget exhaustion, or a hard stop from Plane A fires.
  3. Step 3When evidence_required is true, the conductor must hold last_verify: passed and list evidence paths before advancing past implement tasks—workers cannot clear verification state themselves.
  4. Step 4gates_pending retains HLD, DD, and feature-design waits; A5.2 ensures Continue does not waive them without explicit H1 approval or waiver text in the journal.
  5. Step 5After dual-write, route-tier.py applies model tier and spawn_workers when next_action shifts; pursuit counters that drift from the journal mirror trigger an integrity stop at H2.
