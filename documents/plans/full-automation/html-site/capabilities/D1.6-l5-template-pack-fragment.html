L5 template-pack fragment

L5 is the top rung: template-pack fragments in template-packs/ that ship with company instantiation—default pipelines, role bindings, verify suites, and catalog components new goals inherit on day one. Fragments export upward from consumer repo promotions (D4.6), not one-off edits inside a single product branch.

Pack authors validate fragments against pack schema (Plane F) before publish. An L5 promotion closes the loop started at L0 ephemeral reasoning: the pattern is now organizational capital. Definition-of-done for L5 includes D6.4 graph wiring so downstream goals know when fragments change.

How it works

  1. Step 1Before routing workers to S1+ work each pursuit turn, the conductor and Librarian jointly resolve the needed capability—routing, implement, verify, design artifact, or platform promotion—and name the capability id plus target catalog slice in the briefing.
  2. Step 2Resolution output feeds E2.2 catalog query and E2.3 ranking so compose-first (B4.3) runs at the Plane E protocol layer before improvisation.
  3. Step 3B1.1 preflight may short-circuit resolution when next_action and the task card already bind a catalog component; otherwise Librarian blocks worker spawn until capability lookup completes.
  4. Step 4When resolution names a capability with no catalog match, E2.5 enqueues L0 promotion instead of inventing ad hoc behavior; B4.4 logs divergence if compose is bypassed.
  5. Step 5If capability remains unresolved after catalog search, pursuit stops at H2 with dual-write—never spawn S1+ workers on a vague improvised prompt.
