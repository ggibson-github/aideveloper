Decision H3 scope task milestone release company

Open decision: what granularity triggers H3? Per task, milestone, release, or company-level goal? Finer H3 increases operator load; coarser H3 increases blast radius of acceptance.

Pack authors should declare H3 scope in company.yaml so pursuit knows when to request sign-off.

What this defines

  1. Step 1When B5.1 rotates active_role, the genius-tier conductor retains orchestration identity—merge authority, routing, and sole journal/state dual-write—while each spawn gets a fresh worker persona with role-scoped allowed_reads and tool permissions.
  2. Step 2Workers execute one phase contract (implement, explore, verifier) and return summaries plus evidence paths; they must not assume conductor persistence across turns or mutate journal/state.json even when the active role name changes mid-pursuit.
  3. Step 3Role switches update state.json active_role and pack-derived skill/tool bindings. before the next spawn so a platform worker cannot inherit consumer task scope from the prior persona.
  4. Step 4H2 blocker packaging stays with the conductor at B1.5—workers surface blockers in summaries but do not re-prompt the operator directly, preserving centralized HITL discipline across role rotations.
  5. Step 5If a worker dual-writes journal/state, edits outside role allowed_reads, or the conductor implements large task cards inline when spawn_workers is true, pursuit fails closed at H2 until role context is reset and dual-write authority is restored to the conductor only.
