H1.4 · Persistence & state plane

state, pursuit, and block

state pursuit block

The pursuit block captures mode (continue, goal_autopilot, company_autopilot), step counters, capability_class, last_verify, evidence_files, and gates_pending. Autopilot loops increment steps_total until check-pipeline-blocked stops (A3.1, A3.2).

last_verify: passed is mandatory before advancing past implement tasks when evidence_required is true (G4). gates_pending holds HLD/DD/feature design waits—continue does not clear them (A5.2). route-tier.py applies tier changes when next_action shifts.

How it works

  1. Step 1At H1 or program start, program-scoper selects pack_id.
  2. Step 2Each pursuit wake resolves active_role from the pack.
  3. Step 3Multi-role companies rotate ready workstreams.
  4. Step 4Cross-pack shared libraries import.—organization changes flow through pack updates, not silent consumer-repo forks.
  5. Step 5If pack selection is ambiguous, role mapping conflicts, or work would exceed pack ceiling, Plane F stops at H2 rather than proceeding with mismatched roles, pipelines, or verify suites.
