idle drain product waits H2

When product pursuit waits on external H2—credentials, legal approval, operator answer—CPU and conductor attention would otherwise idle. Idle drain spends that wait on platform queue processing: dequeue and promote without advancing product next_action or clearing gates.

Idle drain respects H2 semantics: no silent gate clearance, no fake implement progress. It maximizes parallel platform evolution during human latency and pairs naturally with autopilot loops that would otherwise spin on blocked checks. When H2 clears, product turns resume on prior next_action; platform backlog should be thinner than if waits were pure no-ops. See A4.1 stop taxonomy.

How it works

  1. Step 1When A4.1 classifies pursuit as blocked on external H2—credentials, legal approval, operator answer—check-pipeline-blocked holds product next_action while conductor attention would otherwise idle.
  2. Step 2Idle drain routes autopilot or /loop turns to dequeue state.platform.promotion_queue items—playbook-keeper, script extraction, catalog rows—without advancing product next_action or clearing gates_pending.
  3. Step 3Platform workers run under conductor spawn contracts with queue item ids; they may not dual-write consumer pursuit state, waive H2, or mark implement tasks complete during idle drain.
  4. Step 4When the operator clears H2, pursuit resumes the frozen next_action and product lanes; journal records how many platform items drained during the wait.
  5. Step 5If idle drain advances product scope, clears blocking_questions, or fakes implement progress while H2 remains open, validate-workflow fails closed and pursuit reverts to the prior blocked state.

Edge cases

  • Operator closes laptop mid-loop — state.json must resume from last good dual-write.
  • Concurrent manual edit to queue JSON — conductor reloads queue each wake; last writer wins with journal note.
  • Platform queue depth 0 but product blocked on missing playbook — D3.3 priority cut skips platform drain.
  • Edge case D3.4 variant 4: verify state dual-write before continuing pursuit.
