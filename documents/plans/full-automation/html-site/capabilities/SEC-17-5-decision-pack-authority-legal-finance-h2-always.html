Decision pack authority legal finance H2-always

Open decision: can packs mark legal/finance roles as fully automated, or must they always escalate H2? Authority-of-record work may never belong in autonomous pursuit regardless of model capability.

Pack schema should encode role_class with automation_allowed flags once decided.

What this defines

  1. Step 1Pack roles in F1.2 may declare role_class (e.g., legal, finance) with automation_allowed—until SEC-17-5 resolves whether authority-of-record roles may ever run without H2.
  2. Step 2When active_role maps to a restricted class, A2.1 preflight treats autonomous implement turns as BLOCKED and forces hitl.pending=H2 before contract, filing, or spend actions proceed.
  3. Step 3B5.1 role switches into legal or finance lanes require manifest or journal-recorded operator approval even under A3.3 company autopilot.
  4. Step 4Economy workers spawned for restricted roles inherit tool allowlists that exclude unsupervised external commits—the conductor cannot waive H2 via Continue alone.
  5. Step 5If pack schema omits automation_allowed for a role or contradicts operator policy, pursuit stops at H2 until pack config or Resolved Q&A records the SEC-17-5 decision—never inferring full autonomy for authority-of-record work.
