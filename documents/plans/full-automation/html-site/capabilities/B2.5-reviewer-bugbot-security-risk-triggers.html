Reviewer bugbot security risk triggers

Reviewer workers run on triggers: Bugbot after substantial implement diffs, security-review when policy flags risk, risk-class controls from Plane G. They are readonly with respect to pursuit state—findings go to the conductor for merge or H2.

Review does not replace verify-router evidence; it adds mistake-class detection before push. Strict mode may require reviewer pass before git-workflow; default self-gate records findings as waivable with expiry when automatable checks pass.

How it works

  1. Step 1After substantial implement diffs or when pack policy flags risk, the conductor spawns readonly reviewer workers—Bugbot for branch changes, security-review for touch paths and dependencies.
  2. Step 2Reviewer findings return as bounded summaries; reviewers do not dual-write pursuit state and cannot clear gates or advance next_action on their own.
  3. Step 3Review complements B2.4 evidence: verify-router proves commands ran; reviewers detect mistake classes from Plane G before push.
  4. Step 4In strict mode the conductor blocks git-workflow until reviewer pass; in default self-gate mode findings may be recorded as waivable with expiry when automatable checks pass.
  5. Step 5Critical or unresolved reviewer findings route to H2 or B1.5 rather than merging to main or requesting H3 sign-off.
