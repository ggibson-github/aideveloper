Conductor genius merge route platform drain

The conductor is genius-tier and thin: merge worker summaries, run S0 preflight, choose next_action, spawn workers, and route platform queue drains—it does not implement large task cards inline when spawn_workers is true.

Each turn may peek at or drain the platform promotion queue on the interleaved product-and-improvement schedule so self-improvement work advances alongside the current project. After workers finish, only the conductor dual-writes journal and state.json. This role separation is the core of genius-conductor-tiered-routing.md.

How it works

  1. Step 1The genius-tier conductor merges worker summaries each turn, reads route-tier output, and chooses next_action without implementing large task cards inline when spawn_workers is true.
  2. Step 2On the interleaved schedule from Plane D, the conductor may peek or drain one platform promotion queue item per K product steps via B4.2 while product pursuit stays on the main stack.
  3. Step 3Only the conductor dual-writes journal/progress.md and journal/state.json after workers return; workers never clear gates, approve design, or advance next_action on their own.
  4. Step 4The conductor spawns economy workers with bounded allowed_reads from task cards or Librarian output and keeps platform workers confined to queue item ids without mutating consumer goal scope.
  5. Step 5If merge would skip S0 preflight, corrupt dual-write, or drain platform work that blocks the active product goal, pursuit stops at H2 until routing and queue policy reconcile.
