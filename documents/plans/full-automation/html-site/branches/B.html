Plane B

Cognition & routing plane

Plane B decides how each turn is executed—deterministic scripts first (S0), economy workers for mechanical work (S1), templated artifacts (S2), architecture judgment (S3), and governance escalation (S4)—with a thin genius-tier conductor.
