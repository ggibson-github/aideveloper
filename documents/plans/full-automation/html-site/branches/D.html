Plane D

Platform evolution plane

Plane D is how the system improves itself while it works—promotion queue, interleaved scheduling so delivery and self-improvement both advance, and maturing reuse from one-off reasoning to template-pack fragments.

This plane has 6 topic groups listed below. Open a group to see its capabilities; sequential groups include a flow diagram.
