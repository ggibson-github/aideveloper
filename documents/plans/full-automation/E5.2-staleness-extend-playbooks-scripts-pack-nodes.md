## Behavior / step logic
<!-- timeline-source: agent cursor-agent 2026-06-28 -->

1. When platform promotion lands playbooks, scripts, or pack fragments, reconcile-stale registers each reusable path in docs/manifest/staleness.json so downstream task cards and compose hits can mark dependencies stale.
2. On script or playbook change during pursuit, the staleness graph propagates to dependent task cards and [E2.2](E2.2-compose-query-catalog-list-components.md) compose results—workers reconcile before advancing implement steps.
3. Regeneration of [E1.1](E1.1-catalog-scripts-manifest-generated.md) and [E1.7](E1.7-catalog-platform-catalog-md-umbrella.md) reconciles graph entries with live catalog INDEX rows after platform drain completes.
4. Pack verify suites from [F1.8](F1.8-pack-verify-goal-verify-suites.md) may assert graph completeness at goal_verify—missing nodes fail closed rather than allowing pursuit on orphan artifact paths.
5. If a promoted artifact lacks a staleness node or graph drift leaves dependents unmarked, pursuit stops at H2 until reconcile-stale or [D4.5](D4.5-platform-work-catalog-regeneration.md) restores traceability.
