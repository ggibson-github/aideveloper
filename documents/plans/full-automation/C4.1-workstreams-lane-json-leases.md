1. During pursuit, when the conductor or a worker runs the identical manual shell command twice—same pytest flags, export script, or validate call—S0 observation records the duplicate and enqueues a promotion candidate into `state.platform.promotion_queue` instead of tolerating endless repetition.

5. If duplicate detection fires without journal provenance, schema validation fails, or enqueue races concurrent manual queue edits, pursuit fails closed at H2 until the conductor dual-writes a conformant queue item and records the duplicate fingerprint.
